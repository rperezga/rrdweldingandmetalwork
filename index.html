Welcome

Who we are

Operating and serving the community for more than 8 years, RRD Welding and Metalwork is the welding company that South Florida counts on. We specializes in all kind of Welding, Metal Fabrication and Manufacturing. Our services include Commercial and Residential Projects. We weld anything and build everything no matter the material.

We just have one mission in mind: top quality metal fabrication, and excellent service. Working with everyone from private homeowners to big corporations. Our work involves but is not limited to fence building, metal repairs, forklift claw extension and many other projects involving metal works.

Offers onsite installation and fabrication, our mobile team is equipped with all the necessary equipment and tools to come to you and work on your project right before your eyes. They are highly skilled technicians who take pride in the quality of our work.
